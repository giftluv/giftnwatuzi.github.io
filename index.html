Biography

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Computer Science at Kent State University. My research focuses on secure and trustworthy artificial intelligence, federated learning, privacy-preserving machine learning, fairness-aware AI, explainability, and secure software systems.

My goal is to design intelligent systems that are reliable, interpretable, privacy-conscious, and suitable for sensitive domains such as healthcare, cybersecurity, and distributed computing environments.

  • Federated Learning and Privacy-Preserving Machine Learning

  • Software Engineering for Secure Intelligent Applications
